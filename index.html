Meet Our Founder

Aisha Rahman

"They told me a mother couldn't build an empire. They were wrong."

Seven years ago, Aisha started Khazana Jewels at her kitchen table while her two children slept. Driven by a passion for accessible luxury and a refusal to compromise on quality, she began designing gold-plated pieces that rivalled fine jewellery.

As a hijabi entrepreneur, she faced closed doors and skepticism, but her relentless spirit turned a small Instagram page into one of India's most beloved premium brands. Today, Khazana Jewels stands as a testament to every woman who dares to dream big—proving that with faith, resilience, and a touch of gold, anything is possible.
